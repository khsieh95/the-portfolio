Hello! My name is Kevin and here's my long story short. After graduating from UMBC and on the brink of attending Physical Therapy school, I chose to start my own tennis coaching business. It grew to be quite successful & being an entrepreneur gave me the luxury of traveling around the United States and Asia. Then covid hit. Then I closed my business and moved from Maryland to Washington. Here I am now, as a Full-stack developer in training with the UW Coding Bootcamp. I am gaining experience with languages from HTML5, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Node.js and many more. I am aiming for the path of a Technical Product Manager where I can combine my passion for tech and entrepreneurship.
